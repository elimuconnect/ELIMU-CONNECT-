Lesson 1: Physical Properties of Metals

  • Lustrous – shiny when polished
  • Good conductors – allow heat and electricity to pass
  • Malleable – hammered into sheets
  • Ductile – drawn into wires

Lesson 2: Composition of Alloys

  • Brass = Copper + Zinc
  • Bronze = Copper + Tin
  • Steel = Iron + Carbon

Lesson 3: Uses of Metals

  • Iron – tools and buildings
  • Aluminum – foil and aircraft
  • Copper – wiring

Lesson 4: Rusting of Iron

Rusting needs water and oxygen.

Prevention methods: Painting, galvanizing, greasing.
