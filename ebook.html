Lesson 4: Rusting of Iron

Rusting is the reaction of iron with oxygen and water forming rust.

Conditions for rusting:

  • Presence of water
  • Presence of air (oxygen)

Prevention Methods:

  • Painting
  • Oiling/Greasing
  • Galvanizing
  • Electroplating

Question:

  1. What are two conditions needed for rusting?
  2. Name three methods of preventing rusting.

Answer: (1) Water and air (2) Painting, galvanizing, electroplating
