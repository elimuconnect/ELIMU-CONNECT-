Lesson 4: Rusting of Iron

Rusting is the reaction of iron with oxygen and water forming rust.

Conditions:

  • Water
  • Oxygen

Prevention:

  • Painting
  • Oiling
  • Galvanizing
  • Electroplating

A: Water and air
